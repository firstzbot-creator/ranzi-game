# Level 1: "The Normal House" (Intro Sequence)

## Overview
This is a small, narrative-driven introductory sequence. Instead of a traditional combat tutorial, it serves as an interactive/animated cutscene that establishes the dramatic stakes of the story, introduces the primary antagonist's forces, and sets the party on their grand adventure.

## Multiplayer Scaling
*   The game supports **1 to 3 players** locally or online.
*   The animation dynamically adjusts to show only the number of cats currently in the party waking up and escaping together.

## Sequential Flow

### Scene 1: Waking Up to Disaster
*   **Visuals**: The scene opens on a cozy, familiar bedroom environment (the "Normal House"). It's late at night. 
*   **Action**: The player(s) wake up abruptly. The room is filling with smoke, and bright orange flames are visible licking at the doorframe. The house is completely engulfed in fire.
*   **Player Control**: Minimal. The players may only be able to walk/run towards the exit, unable to gather any belongings or weapons.

### Scene 2: The Tragic Escape
*   **Action**: The cats scramble through the burning hallways, dodging falling debris. 
*   **Story Beat**: It becomes devastatingly clear that the humans in the house succumb to the sudden and intense fire. The cats can do nothing to save them; they must focus purely on escaping with their own lives (and ensuring their friends make it out). They manage to squeeze out of a broken window or pet door just as the structure begins to collapse.

### Scene 3: The Cackling Lava Monster
*   **Visuals**: Outside on the lawn, illuminated by the tragic glow of the burning house.
*   **Action**: A massive, terrifying **Lava Monster** emerges from the flames, towering over the small cats.
*   **Dialogue**: The Lava Monster cackles maniacally and delivers the inciting monologue:
    > "Foolish felines! You thought you could hide from the Dog King?! His grand plan has begun, and every last Relic shall be exterminated!"
*   **Revelation**: This is the moment the cats (and the player) learn they are not just normal pets, but targeted "Relics."

### Scene 4: The Flight
*   **Action**: Terrified by the monstrous sight and the immense tragedy, the cats turn and flee into the surrounding woods or neighborhood.
*   **Transition**: The screen fades to black as they run into the night, officially concluding the prologue and starting the grand adventure in the first real biome.
