# Level 1: "The Gloomy Forest"

## Overview
This is the very first playable level immediately following the tragic burning of the "Normal House." The players (the cats) are traumatized, weak, and stripped of everything. This level serves as the mechanical tutorial for survival and class selection, culminating in the first boss fight.

## Survival Phase (First 5 Minutes)
*   **Atmosphere**: Dark, foreboding, but with small pockets of cozy lighting (like glowing mushrooms or stray moonlight).
*   **Player Status**: The cats start with a critically low health bar and low stamina. They are in desperate need of food and water to survive.
*   **The Tutorial**: Pop-ups or subtle visual cues guide the player through the core survival mechanics:
    *   **Find Water**: Locating a small, clean stream to replenish hydration.
    *   **Find Food**: Scavenging for berries, safe mushrooms, or catching a small (non-magical) bug.
    *   **Cooking & Building**: A brief tutorial on setting up a tiny, crude campfire using sticks to cook the scavenged food, restoring the cats to full health.

## The Cave & Class Selection
*   **The Guiding Light**: After exactly five minutes of surviving and exploring the immediate area, a magical, glowing arrow (perhaps left by another ancient Relic or formed by fireflies) appears, beckoning the party deeper into the forest.
*   **The Cave**: The arrow leads them into a mysterious, ancient cave adorned with magical runes.
*   **The Choice**: Inside, four glowing pedestals stand in a circle. Each cat (player) must approach a pedestal to unlock their magical potential and choose their base element:
    1.  **Fire** (High damage, burns, synergy via "Wildfire")
    2.  **Water** (Healing, synergy via "Boil")
    3.  **Wind** (Movement speed, pushing enemies back)
    4.  **Air/Lightning** (Fast, chained attacks) 
*   *Note: Other elements (Cloud, Earth, Sand, Ice, etc.) will be discovered or unlocked much later in the game as the adventure progresses.*

## The Preparation
*   **Testing Grounds**: After selecting their element, the cats are given a small window of time (perhaps 2 to 3 minutes) inside the safe confines of the cave.
*   **Purpose**: This allows players to test out their new Level 1 spells (like "Sparks" for the Fire Wizard), figure out their magic cooldowns, and perhaps cook one more meal for a buff.

## The First Boss: The Evil Slime
*   **The Ambusher**: Just as the preparation time ends, the cave entrance is suddenly blocked by a massive, pulsing, corrupt enemy: **An Evil Slime**.
*   **The Fight**: It is the first true test of combat. The Slime is relatively slow but hits hard, forcing players to use their new elemental spells, manage their 20-second cooldowns, and practice dodging. It introduces the players to the core action-adventure combat loop they will use throughout the game.
