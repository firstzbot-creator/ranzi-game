# Fire Wizard Cat: Class Design

## Overview
The Fire Wizard Cat specializes in high damage, damage-over-time (burns), and incredibly powerful synergistic spells. Their spells often require careful timing due to a universal 20-second cooldown per spell.

## Mechanics
*   **Base Cooldown**: Every spell cast requires a **20-second recharge** before it can be used again. Strategic use of spacing and dodging is required while waiting for cooldowns.

## Spell Progression (Level 1 - Level 10)

| Level | Spell Name | Type | Damage | Effect / Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Lv 1** | **Sparks** | Ranged Attack | 10 | A basic magical spark attack to initiate combat. |
| **Lv 2** | **Ember** | Ranged Combo | 20 (60 Combo) | Shoots a hot ember. **Wildfire Synergy**: If two Fire Wizard Cats cast Ember at the exact same time, it combines into a massive "Wildfire" dealing 60 damage! |
| **Lv 3** | **Roar** | Wave Attack | 30 | Unleashes a small wave of fire that travels toward the enemy, capable of hitting multiple targets in a line. |
| **Lv 4** | **Blaze** | Damage over Time | 40 (over time) | Ignites the enemy, causing them to temporarily burn for 10 seconds, dealing 40 total damage over the duration. |
| **Lv 5** | **Blister** | Debuff (Slow) | 0 | Severely blisters the enemy, making them weak and significantly slowing their movement for 15 seconds. |
| **Lv 6** | **Tornado** | Crowd Control | 0 | Summons a fiery whirlwind that traps the enemy, forcing them to stay completely still for 15 seconds. |
| **Lv 7** | **Burning Heal** | High-Risk Nuke | 100 | **Cost:** Decreases entire team's health by 10. <br>**Effect:** Blasts the enemy with an overpowering burst of heat for 100 damage. |
| **Lv 8** | **Boil** | Elemental Synergy | 0 (Alone) | Does absolutely no damage on its own. **Evaporate Synergy**: If a Water Wizard casts a water spell simultaneously, it causes massive evaporation, completely melting/evaporating the enemy. |
| **Lv 9** | **Burning Decrease** | Support & Attack | 80 | A magical flare that strangely invigorates allies. Heals the user and the team by 10 HP, while simultaneously harming the enemy for 80 damage. |
| **Lv 10** | **Cannon** | Ultimate Attack | 100 | Summons a colossal fire bomb that explodes, dealing a massive 100 damage in a huge area. |
