# Game Design Document: Ranzi Game (Working Title)

## 1. Core Concept
A cozy, storybook-style 2D pixel art adventure where players control a party of customizable feline wizards. After their normal house is destroyed by a monster, the cats discover they are ancient magical "relics." They must embark on an epic quest across diverse, surreal biomes to defeat the Evil Dog King and his army of corrupted everyday objects and rival animals.

## 2. Visual Style & Vibe
*   **Aesthetic**: Cozy, highly-detailed pixel art styled like a living storybook.
*   **Color Palette**: Soft pastel colors contrasted by vibrant, glowing elemental magic effects.
*   **Atmosphere**: Begins quaint and domestic, evolving into a grand, magical, yet consistently cozy adventure.

## 3. Story & World
*   **The Hook**: The tutorial begins in a seemingly normal house. A powerful monster suddenly attacks and destroys the home. In the rubble, the cats awaken their dormant powers, realizing they are ancient, magical "relics".
*   **The Antagonist**: The overarching villain is the Evil Dog King, who commands the monsters plaguing the land.
*   **Enemies & "Everyday Monsters"**: The enemies are warped, aggressive versions of things cats would naturally encounter:
    *   Angry neighborhood dogs.
    *   Vicious, magically-animated litter boxes.
    *   Possessed vacuum cleaners or spraying water bottles.
    *   Rival cats aligned with the Dog King.
*   **Biomes**: The journey is structured level-by-level across drastically different, magical biomes. Examples include:
    *   The Enchanted Forest
    *   The Scorching Sand-Dunes
    *   **The Undersea Kingdom** (Atlantis-style aquatic ruins)
    *   **The Sky Places** (floating cloud cities and celestial temples)

## 4. Gameplay Mechanics
*   **Feline Wizards**: All playable cats are spellcasters. Players can customize their cats to master specific elemental magic skill trees, including:
    *   Fire, Wind, Earth, Water, Cloud, Sand, Ice, and more.
*   **Progression & Achievements**: As the party clears levels and defeats enemies, they level up.
    *   Leveling up unlocks **new, more powerful spells** in their chosen elemental tree.
    *   Players unlock **Achievements** for exploring off the beaten path, defeating bosses, or mastering cooking recipes.
*   **Core Systems**:
    *   **Fight**: Action-adventure combat using elemental magic, combos, and acquired equipment.
    *   **Explore**: Traversing levels to find secrets, lore, and defeat area bosses.
    *   **Build & Cook**: Between levels, players can set up camp, build out a safe zone, and cook meals using ingredients found in the biomes to grant buffs.
*   **Dynamic Weather**: Weather events heavily impact gameplay and elemental magic. 
    *   *Examples*: Rain puts out fire spells but boosts water; Sandstorms reduce visibility; Heat Waves drain stamina.
*   **Party & Survival Mechanics**:
    *   Each cat has exactly **3 lives** per level or checkpoint.
    *   **Co-op & Healing**: Cats can use specific spells, items, or cooked meals to heal one another to avoid losing a precious life.
